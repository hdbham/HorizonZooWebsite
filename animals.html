Want to know more?

Go behind-the-scenes and interact with some of the most fascinating animals in the world! Book your encounter now to learn about or animals, how we care for them, our efforts to conserve their species, and how you can help!

The mission of the Horizon Zoo animal experiences is to engage and inspire our community to better understand and preserve wildlife by creating meaningful connections between people and animals.There's no better way to gain a connection with our animals than to interact with them in their exhibit.

Online reservations are required to schedule an encounter. All encounters must be booked at least 48 hours in advance. All encounters are 30-minute programs and allow up to six participants. All persons must be 7 years of age or older and all persons under the age of 13 must be accompanied by a paying adult. Prices of the encounters range from $75 to $200

Animal encounter participants are required to:

  • wear closed-toed shoes
  • wear gloves provided by the Zoo
  • sign an animal encounter waiver

Each animal encounter program includes:

  • behind-the-scenes access
  • animal interactions
  • opportunities to learn from our zookeepers & educators
  • photos taken by zoo staff on a camera phone provided by the participant

Below are some of our most popular animal encounters

Tremendous Tortoises Encounter

During this experience guests will meet Frankie, a 75-year-old Galapagos tortoise. You will be able to see, feed, and touch Frankie along with his family and friends. Some are shy, while others are more interactive with their keepers, stretching up for a neck rub or eating right out of their hand. Tortoises are attracted to the colors found in flowers, a favorite food—reds, yellows, pinks, and oranges. Try wearing your brightest clothing and see if you get the attention of a Galapagos tortoise!

Giant Giraffe Encouunter

Stroll behind-the-scenes of the Giraffe Barn and stand toe-to-toe with the Zoo’s tallest residents—the Masai giraffe! Marvel at their long eyelashes and even longer purple tongue as they grasp their favorite fresh leaves from your waiting hand. Lettuce or other treats will be provided by the zoo staff. Skilled animal care staff will be on hand to educate you about giraffe behavior, nutrition and breeding practices. Don’t miss this mesmerizing experience! Portions of this tour are ADA Accessible.

Elephant Extravaganza

The Elephant Extravaganza includes an opportunity to meet face-to-trunk with Anna, one of our Asian elephant cows. Help our elephant staff wash Anna and learn about her daily care and upkeep. Watch Anna paint a masterpiece that you can take home and place on the mantle to remind you of your special day. The staff will share information on the threats facing African and Asian elephants in the wild and steps all visitors can take to aid in their conservation.

Hangin' With Hippos

During this experience, care team members will lead groups around Hippo Cove to get a closer ook at what it takes to care for our hippos. Groups will have the opportunity to see the hippo habitat inside the hippo building as well as the care team’s prep areas, including the kitchen space. After viewing the indoor areas, groups will venture outside where they will have the opportunity to meet the hippos and watch them participate in a training session. Our hippos are managed through protected contact so groups will not have the opportunity to touch the hippos during the tour.

Chatting With Cheetahs

Did you know cheetahs are the largest cats who can purr?! Come and hear for yourself! During your Chatting with Cheetahs encounter, you’ll get close enough to hear, see and smell the Zoo’s quickest residents! From diets and dishes to training sessions and enrichment, guests will get to see everything that goes into caring for these fast felines. We can’t wait to have you help us conserve this endangered species and form a connection with our cheetahs.

World of Reptiles

Come face to face with reptile giants, watch hatching and newborn reptiles being nurtured and raised, and immerse yourself in a floor to ceiling marsh habitat of the Siamese crocodile. Learn about animal care, behavior and conservation on an up-close and personal behind the scenes tour! Be introduced to a zoo keeper who can share information about the animals they care for as only they know them!
